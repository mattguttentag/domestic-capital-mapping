# Mediterrania Capital / CDG Research Note - 2026-06-25

## Question

Does public evidence support retaining `Mediterrania Capital II / III / IV` as a confirmed African pension fund / sovereign wealth fund commitment through CDG Morocco?

## Finding

No. Public sources confirm that Mediterrania Capital II, III and IV are real funds with DFI participation, but I did not find a source that identifies Caisse de Depot et de Gestion (CDG), CDG Invest, CDG Capital, or CDG Invest Growth as an LP in any specific Mediterrania Capital fund.

The evidence found for CDG is transaction-level co-investment, not fund-LP participation. In particular, 2025 public sources show Mediterrania Capital Partners and CDG Invest Growth investing alongside each other in Dislog Dispositifs Medicaux. That is not enough to classify CDG as an LP in Mediterrania II, III, or IV.
